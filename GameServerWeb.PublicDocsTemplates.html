    GameServerWeb.PublicDocsTemplates (game_server v1.0.272)

    Embedded HEEx templates used by the public docs LiveView.

    This module wraps embed_templates "public_docs/*" so templates can be referenced from GameServerWeb.PublicDocs and keeps large static pages cleanly separated into smaller .heex partials.
